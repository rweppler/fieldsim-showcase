How it works

YOUR LINEUP IS A DISTRIBUTION, NOT A NUMBER.

Because one number never won a milly

01
Contest generator

Build the field you'll actually face

Projected ownership is treated as ground truth — never renormalized, never smoothed. The generator fills 10,000 valid DraftKings rosters and calibrates until every player's exposure lands within a hard tolerance band of their projection. Scarce positions fill first, so the salary squeeze falls where real fields feel it.

02
Contest simulator

Play the tournament 50,000 times

Every player gets a right-skewed distribution fitted to their mean, volatility, ceiling and boom/bust odds. A Gaussian copula ties teammates, stacks and game environments together, then the engine scores the full field 50,000 times, pays out a top-heavy GPP curve — with duplication splitting prizes — and reports each lineup's ROI.

THE CHALK TAX

Popular lineups get duplicated. SimSlate models expected duplicates for every field lineup from joint ownership, so a chalky build that "wins" shares first place — and its simulated ROI shows the dilution before you pay entry fees to find out.

corr < 0

THE LEVERAGE GUARANTEE

Within every projected-score decile, simulated ROI is verified to correlate negatively with ownership. Lower-owned paths to the same score are worth more — that's the math of top-heavy payouts, and the engine won't ship a sim where it doesn't hold.
