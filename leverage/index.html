The decile law

Sort ten thousand simulated lineups into deciles by projected score, so that every panel below compares lineups of equal quality. Then, inside each panel, plot ownership against realized ROI. The engine enforces what tournament history keeps proving: the slope is negative in every single decile. Skill picks the decile. Ownership picks who gets paid for it.
